“Meaning is the product of interaction between the observer and the system, the content of which is in a state of flux, of endless change and transformation.”1

Myron Krueger with his Responsive Enviornments approaches space as an interactive enviornment, where the relationship between the user and the machine is paramount, Ted Nelson with Computer Lib/Dreams Machines argues that the link between humans and digital machines provides a different meaning in the world of interaction, it creates Information Skies.
